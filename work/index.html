Noodles

Discription

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Vegetables
  • spices
  • Eggs

STEPS

  1. Boil water till its a bit warm.But not too warm
  2. Break your noodles into warm water and cover
  3. After 3mins put in spices and stir a bit
  4. Put in vegetables right after the spices
  5. Followed with the Eggs but dont stir
  6. Allow eggs and vegetables to cook a bit with the eggs not scrambled.

This is instant noodles.Easy to prepare and tasty.
